About LIC

The Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) was founded in 1956 with the mission to provide life insurance to every Indian citizen. With decades of trust, LIC stands as one of the most reliable institutions in India.

Our vision is to enhance the quality of life of people through financial security by providing products and services of aspired attributes with competitive returns.
